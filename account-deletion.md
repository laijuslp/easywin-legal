# Account Deletion

_Last updated: January 22, 2026_

## How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your EasyWin account directly from within the app:

1. Go to your profile settings.
2. Select “Delete Account.”
3. Confirm your choice.

## What Happens After Deletion

- Your personal data and learning history will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.
- You will lose access to all app features and content.
- Some data may be retained if required by law.

## Need Help?

If you have trouble deleting your account, contact [support@easywin.com](mailto:support@easywin.com).
