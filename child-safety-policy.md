# Child Safety & Age Restriction Policy – EasyWin

**Last updated: 21 January 2026**

EasyWin is an educational application intended for users **aged 13 years and above**. The app is **not designed for children under the age of 13**, and EasyWin does not knowingly allow such users to create or maintain accounts.

## Age Restriction
EasyWin is intended for:
- Students aged **13 years and above**
- Teenagers and adults using the app for educational and learning purposes
- Teachers and educators using the app as a learning support tool
- Parents or guardians assisting learners
- Individuals preparing for employment-related learning or competitive examinations


Children under 13 years of age are **not permitted** to use EasyWin.

## No Intentional Data Collection from Children
EasyWin does **not knowingly collect, store, or process personal data from children under 13**. If it is discovered that a user under 13 has created an account or provided personal data:
- The account will be terminated
- Associated data will be deleted promptly

Parents or guardians may contact EasyWin to request deletion of such data.

## Account Creation Safeguards
To help prevent underage use:
- Users are required to confirm eligibility during account creation
- Age-related information may be used solely for compliance purposes

## Advertising and Monetization
EasyWin does not target children with advertising.  
All monetization features, if present, are designed for users aged 13+ and comply with Google Play policies.

## AI-Assisted Features
EasyWin may use AI-assisted tools to support learning. These features are:
- Designed for general educational assistance
- Not intended for use by children under 13
- Not a substitute for professional or academic supervision

## Third-Party Services
Any third-party services or SDKs used by EasyWin:
- Are limited to essential functionality (such as analytics or crash reporting)
- Are not authorized to collect data from children
- Are selected to comply with applicable data protection laws

## Reporting Underage Use
If a parent, guardian, or user believes that a child under 13 is using EasyWin, they should contact EasyWin immediately so appropriate action can be taken.

## Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
