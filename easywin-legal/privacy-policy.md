# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: January 22, 2026_

## Introduction

EasyWin is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our Android app.

## Information We Collect

- **Personal Information:** Name, email address, and profile details you provide.
- **Usage Data:** App usage statistics, device information, and interaction logs.
- **Learning Data:** Progress, scores, and activity within the app.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and improve our educational services.
- To personalize your learning experience.
- To communicate important updates and support.
- To comply with legal obligations.

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on trusted servers.
- We use encryption and access controls to protect your information.
- Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described.

## Sharing of Information

- We do not sell your personal information.
- Data may be shared with service providers for app functionality, under strict confidentiality.
- We may disclose information if required by law.

## User Rights

- You can access, update, or delete your personal information within the app.
- You may request data export or deletion by contacting our support team.

## Children’s Privacy

EasyWin is designed for educational use. Users under 18 should use the app with parental guidance.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
