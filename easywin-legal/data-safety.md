# Data Safety

_Last updated: January 22, 2026_

## Overview

EasyWin is committed to protecting your data and complying with Google Play’s Data Safety requirements.

## Data Collected

- Personal information (name, email)
- App usage data
- Learning progress and activity

## Data Usage

- To provide and personalize educational services
- For analytics and app improvement
- For customer support

## Data Sharing

- Data is not sold to third parties.
- Shared only with trusted service providers for app functionality.

## Data Security

- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel.

## User Control

- Users can access, update, or delete their data in the app.
- Account deletion removes personal data from our systems within 30 days.

## Children’s Data

- The app is suitable for students in India.
- Parental guidance is recommended for users under 18.

## Contact

For data safety concerns, email [support@easywin.com](mailto:support@easywin.com).
