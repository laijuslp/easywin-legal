# AI Disclosure

_Last updated: January 22, 2026_

## Use of Artificial Intelligence

EasyWin uses artificial intelligence to assist with learning recommendations, content personalization, and progress tracking.

## How AI Is Used

- Suggesting study materials based on your activity
- Providing feedback and hints
- Analyzing learning patterns to improve your experience

## Limitations

- AI suggestions are for guidance only and may not always be accurate.
- Human review is recommended for important decisions.
- The app does not make high-stakes decisions about users.

## Transparency

We are committed to responsible AI use and regularly review our systems for fairness and accuracy.
